Labels & pills

.lbl label .tag .meta-pill .navsection

All mono, uppercase, weight 400, 0.05em letter-spacing. .lbl/.tag are 13px, filters/pills are 12px.

Buttons

One canonical button — .lpbtn — with --primary (solid bright blue), --secondary (outline), plus --sm and --full modifiers. Mono, uppercase, 13px, 0.05em, 44px tall (36px for --sm). Preferred CTAs lead with a corner-down-right arrow.

Links

Inline link · .lplink In body copy: read the docs for details.

Inline text links use .lplink (royal, underline on hover). Navigation affordances ("View …") are .lpbtn buttons, not text links. Sidebar nav, the top-bar breadcrumb (.lp-crumb), and footer columns keep their own contextual link styles.
